Welcome

Hi! I'm Dania, a game design and development student at RIT. I'm working on this personal project that brings together my passion for animation and games. While I'm still exploring different game genres, I'm already shaping the visuals and tone inspired by the things I love. My influences include the charming world of the Kirby series and the creative styles of some of my favorite artists, like Amelicart and Art with Em. Stay tuned!

Project

While working on this project, I've encountered some hurdles that have challenged my creative process. As I dive deeper into animation and game development, I'm focusing on overcoming these challenges to bring my vision to life. Here are some specific areas I'm currently navigating:

  1. Collage/Crayon Style: I've found it difficult to replicate a collage or crayon-like aesthetic digitally, so I'm developing my own custom brushes in Photoshop to achieve those effects.
  2. Game Genre Exploration: I'm having a tough time settling on a specific game genre. To ensure I make an informed choice, I'm keeping my options open and planning to conduct thorough research on different genres.
  3. Story Development: I'm also working to finalize the general story behind the game, as I want to ensure it aligns well with the overall tone and visuals I'm aiming for.

Goals

I'm excited to take on this project as a chance to expand my skills in Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Animate while exploring my artistic style. I'm also stepping out of my comfort zone by using Godot for the first time. Through this experience, I aim to refine my technical abilities, experiment with new tools, and push my creative boundaries. I'll also be developing my project management skills and embracing creative problem-solving along the way. I can't wait to see how this project helps me grow as both an artist and a developer!
