Welcome

Hi! I'm Dania, a game design and development student at RIT. I'm working on this personal project that brings together my passion for animation and games. While I'm still exploring different game genres, I'm already shaping the visuals and tone inspired by the things I love. My influences include the charming world of the Kirby series and the creative styles of some of my favorite artists, like Amelicart and Art with Em. Stay tuned!

Project

While working on this project, I've encountered some hurdles that have challenged my creative process. As I dive deeper into animation and game development, I'm focusing on overcoming these challenges to bring my vision to life. Here are some specific areas I'm currently navigating:

  1. Art Style: I'm developing my own custom brushes in Photoshop to achieve brush strokes similar to PictoChat.
  2. Game Genre: I'm having a tough time settling on a specific game genre so I'm keeping my options open and planning to research different genres.
  3. Story Development: I'm also working to finalize the general story behind the game, as I want to ensure it aligns well with the overall tone and visuals I'm aiming for.

Audience

This game is crafted for young children and casual gamers who seek simple, enjoyable gameplay without complex mechanics. Planning to offer a fun, friendly experience that encourages exploration and creativity, making it ideal for players looking to unwind and play at their own pace. Look forward to any upcoming updates!

Goals

I'm excited to take on this project as a chance to expand my skills in Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Animate while exploring my artistic style. I'm also stepping out of my comfort zone by using Godot for the first time. Through this experience, I aim to refine my technical abilities, experiment with new tools, and push my creative boundaries.
