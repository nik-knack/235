Concept

Shelter Cat is based on tamagotchi. The player will play as an animal shelter volunteer where they must take care of their selected shelter cat to earn "cute" points. The bigger the number of "cute" points, the more likely the cat will find a new home with a loving owner.

Genre

Vitual Pet Simulation

Platform

Desktop only

Story

As a new animal shelter volunteer, you must learn to take care of a selected cat to give the cat a bigger positibility of getting adopted.

Aesthetics

Pixel art with limited color palette similar to the super gameboy. Sound design will also be 8-bit sfx and music.

Gameplay

The player will take care of their selected cat through grooming and enrichment activities such as brushing, feeding, playing.
