Concept

Shelter Cat is based on tamagotchi. The player will play as an animal shelter volunteer where they must take care of their selected shelter cat to earn "cute" points. The bigger the number of "cute" points, the more likely the cat will find a new home with a loving owner.

Genre

Vitual Pet Simulation

Platform

Desktop only

Story

As a new animal shelter volunteer, you must learn to take care of a selected cat to give the cat a bigger positibility of getting adopted.

Aesthetics

Pixel art with limited color palette similar to the super gameboy. Sound design will also be 8-bit sfx and music.

Gameplay

The player will take care of their selected cat through grooming and enrichment activities such as brushing, feeding, playing.

About Me

Hello! My name is Dania and I'm a second year game design and development student interested in programming and art. Some of my skills are: C#, HTML/CSS, Javascript, Photoshop, Animate, Fresco, Godot.

Notes

I believe this project has helped me learn about the process of development at my own pace. For this project I made my own 2D assets. I found PixiJS to be simple to use as it reminded of MonoGame. The most challending part was rearranging my code to be as legible as possible and getting my buttons class to work properly the first time. Overall, I enjoyed this project and will probably use PixiJS again in the future. I've linked the resources for all my sounds in the following section.
